  1. Candidates shall occupy the seats allotted to them in the Examination Hall before the time specified on the Admission Ticket. LATE COMERS WILL NOT BE ADMITTED TO THE EXAMINATION.
  2. Candidates shall bring Admission Ticket, Identity proof and ball point pen with blue or black ink into Examination Hall. Candidates shall not bring with them any electronic/communication device such as Calculator, Mobile Phone, Digital Diary etc. in the Examination Hall. Usage of smart watch is strictly prohibited in the Examination Hall.
  3. Candidates shall put their signature at the bottom side, across the photo in the address list provided in the Examination Hall. DO NOT SIGN OVER THE FACE.
  4. Candidates should produce a photo affixed Identity Proof in original. Only the following 5 documents will be accepted as identity proof. 1) Voters ID issued by Election Commission 2) Driving Licence 3) ID Card issued by Social Welfare Dept. of Govt. of Kerala to Differently Abled persons 4) Photo affixed Passbook issued by Nationalised Banks 5) PAN Card. Candidates who fail to produce Identity Proof in original will not be permitted to attend the examination.
  5. Candidates should not misbehave with the officials engaged in examination duty or commit any sort of malpractice or resort to any unfair means during the course of the examination. The answer scripts of the candidates who violate the above instructions will be invalidated and legal action will be taken against them.
  6. No change of examination centre will be allowed under any circumstances.
